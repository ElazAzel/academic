Core conclusion mapping
1. Sell as closed operational academy, not generic LMS
   Evidence: local platform overview, role model, customer observer, curator model, risks, reports, certificates.

2. Best ICP is B2B closed academy / corporate L&D / project education operator
   Evidence: product strengths align with cohort, curator, reporting and certificate workflows.

3. Avoid head-on replacement of university LMS and K-12 platforms
   Evidence: Platonus/Univer/Moodle already used in higher education; BilimLand/OnlineMektep strong in school/content ecosystems.

4. Gov production readiness is not yet achieved
   Evidence: READINESS partial, DPA gaps, Supabase password rotation, hosting/data residency uncertainty, no formal attestation/control evidence.

5. Pilot path is realistic after P1 blockers
   Evidence: local functionality is broad, but legal/security/ops evidence needs closure before formal tender.
